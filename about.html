About WE Improv

I love performing and teaching improv, and I wanted to create a curriculum that reflected that. These are small-group opportunities to become great team players that are targeted, consistent, affordable, and mirror a house team experience. You’ll get the chance to learn new forms and hone your skill through lots of reps and a show for charity!

A little about me: I've been teaching since 2010 and coaching since 2012. I've taught collegiate workshops in San Diego, San Luis Obispo, and Olympia. Last year I took my improv podcast, The MEAT, on a nine city tour, teaching workshops in Phoenix, New Orleans, Washington D.C. and Philadelphia.

I was a 2010 Teach For America Corps Member, and I earned my Masters in Education Strategies from LMU. I was an educational specialist for seven years in Los Angeles.

I'm on the UCB Harold Team Mondegreen, the form inventing team Puddy, and the set stealing Copycat. I'm the co-creator and co-host of The MEAT Improv Podcast alongside Josh Simpson, and the monthly late night talk show We're Gross with Gilli Nissim.

Ok that’s it. Let’s make some great improv together!
