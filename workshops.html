Indie Harold Workshops

This workshop is designed around teaching the skills and techniques to perform a fun abridged Harold within the an indie time slot.
We'll be focusing on all the parts of a Harold (opening, initiations, second beats, support, groups games, heightening) and doing it in 15 minutes! It consists of four workshops and three shows with a max class size of 7.
$165
$20 discount if you are referred or refer someone or if you've taken a workshop with me before.
Classes on Sundays from 3-5pm
Shows will be Tuesday and Wednesday nights at 10pm.
Location: Moving Arts.
